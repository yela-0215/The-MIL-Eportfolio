Anticipating Hurdles & Mitigating Them

Managing my media consumption has been a bit tricky for me. I tend to dive into a spiral of overthinking and over-analyzing everything. It's like my mind goes into overdrive with thoughts like, "Maybe this individual is dealing with this issue" or something similar when I come across certain themes in media. It's like I get caught up in trying to dissect every element.

To address this, I think setting some boundaries on how much media I consume and being more mindful of what I'm exposing myself to could be helpful. Maybe taking breaks and consciously choosing content that's more positive and uplifting could shift my focus away from constant analysis.
