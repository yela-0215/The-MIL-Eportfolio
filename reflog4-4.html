Anticipating Hurdles & Mitigating Them

When using audio information and media, challenges include technical issues, ensuring accuracy and credibility, navigating copyright, addressing accessibility, and adapting to evolving technologies and trends.

To prevent or address challenges when using audio information and media, ensure regular equipment maintenance, conduct thorough research for accuracy, obtain necessary permissions for content use, provide accessibility features like captions, and stay updated through ongoing learning opportunities.
